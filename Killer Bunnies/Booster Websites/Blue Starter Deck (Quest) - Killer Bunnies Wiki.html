The Blue Starter Deck is the starter deck for Killer Bunnies and the Quest for the Magic Carrot. It contains cards 001 - 110, six 12-sided dice (violet, orange, green, yellow, blue, and black), 12 cabbage cards, 12 water cards, and carrot cards 01 - 08. It also usually (always?) includes the Yellow Booster Deck.
