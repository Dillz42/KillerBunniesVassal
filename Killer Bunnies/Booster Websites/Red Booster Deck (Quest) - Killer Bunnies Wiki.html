Red Booster Deck (Quest)
Deck Info
Cards 166 - 220
Features 4 Carrots, Defense, Barriers
Dice
Shop Rooney's Weapons Emporium
◄ Prev Yellow
Next ► Violet

The Red Booster Deck is the second booster deck for Killer Bunnies and the Quest for the Magic Carrot, between the Yellow Booster Deck and the Violet Booster Deck. It contains cards 166 - 220, a red 12-sided die, 1 cabbage card, 1 water card, 6 defense cards, and carrot cards 13 - 16.
