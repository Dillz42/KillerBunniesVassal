Featured Article

The Journey to Jupiter

Killer Bunnies and the Journey to Jupiter, or J2J to its fans, is the second of three Killer Bunnies games designed by Jeffrey Neil Bellinger and published by Playroom Entertainment. It is a non-collectible game, featuring many of the of the game mechanics found in Killer Bunnies and the Quest for the Magic Carrot.
