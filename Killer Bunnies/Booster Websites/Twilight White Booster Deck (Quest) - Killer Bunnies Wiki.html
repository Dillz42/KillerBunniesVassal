The Twilight White Booster Deck is the sixth Booster Deck for Killer Bunnies and the Quest for the Magic Carrot, between the Green Booster Deck and the Stainless Steel Booster Deck. It contains cards 386 - 440, a white 12-sided die and two additional pawns (white and black).
